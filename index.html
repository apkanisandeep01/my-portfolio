// about_me

I didn't start with a keyboard.
I started with Microsoft Excel and a broken system.

For 8 years I worked as an Agriculture Extension Officer for the Government of Telangana, managing data for over 50,000+ farmers across Vikarabad District. Every morning started with Excel sheets, paper forms, and the question: "Why is this taking so long?"

So I started automating. I built Python tools to update farmer databases, automated government portal workflows with Selenium, and created data pipelines that saved my department hours every week. Nobody asked me to. I just couldn't stand inefficiency.

That drive brought me to the UK on the Young India Professionals Scheme — a competitive visa programme that selects professionals committed to building careers in Britain. I'm fully work-authorised, UK-based, and ready to contribute from day one.

I'm now enrolled in Scaler Academy's Data Science & Machine Learning programme, sharpening my SQL, Python, Tableau and Power BI skills with a portfolio built around real datasets — from Walmart Black Friday sales patterns to UK Reatil price trends.

What makes me different isn't just the technical toolkit. It's that I've sat on the other side of the data — I've been the end user who needed the insight, not just the person who built the dashboard. That context makes my analysis actually useful.
